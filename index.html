What is DDDC?

Doki Doki Date Club! is a mod for Doki Doki Literature Club designed with the intention of providing an experience more in line with traditional romance visual novels like Katawa Shoujo. The mod will have a story branch for each of the four girls that will expand their backstories and personalities, giving you the chance to spend more time with your favourite club member! The path to love and literature wont be an easy one, though. Will you write the way into her heart, or will you break it?
Doki Doki Date Club is a fan-made mod created by Team SilverJack, based around the idea of turning the original game into a regular Visual Novel. This is not a commercial project by any means, and it’s completely free for distribution. The original game - Doki Doki Literature Club - is property of its rightful owners, Team Salvato.
